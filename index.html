Workflow

From Concept to Completion: My Process in Action

01.

UX Research

I approach UX research with a user-centered mindset, ensuring that every design decision is backed by data and real user insights.

My process begins with understanding the target audience through competitor analysis, user interviews, and surveys. I then create user personas and journey maps to identify weak points and opportunities for improvement.

02.

Wireframe and Prototyping

I start by defining the information architecture and layout through low-fidelity wireframes, ensuring a logical and seamless user flow.

Using Figma, I create interactive prototypes to simulate real user experiences, allowing for early testing and feedback.

This iterative process ensures that the final design is both functional and visually compelling before development begins.

03.

Design

Finally, I integrate all the previous steps to create a detailed and well-considered design, informed by thorough research and insights. For each client, I typically present 2 design options, allowing them to choose the one that best aligns with their vision.

The design process generally takes between 1 to 2 weeks to complete, with smaller projects requiring only a few days for finalization.

04.

Development

Once the design is finalized, I move into the development phase, translating the design into a fully functional website.

I write clean, efficient code, ensuring cross-browser compatibility, optimal performance, and responsiveness across all devices.

The coding process involves implementing front-end technologies such as HTML, CSS and JavaScript, as well as back-end functionality where needed.
